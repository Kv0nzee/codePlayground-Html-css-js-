About the Game

"Tank Crews offer the players an experience to be part of a working tank crew. Play with your friends even from the other side of the world through voice chat. Take control of a specific role, command, shoot or drive the vehicle to ensure your crews' survival and the destruction of your enemies."

FAQ

How realistic is the game?

Tank Crews offer realistic shell penetration calculation and ballistic calculation in order to simulate real battles between several tanks. Although, all of them will to be toned down to be fun

I want to learn about the details of the tanks, but where??

We are planning to add multiple vehicles into the game itself. So, the amount of content could be overwhelming for you. So, we have made a wiki system where you can check the details of each vehicle, including the positions of the crews, modifications' positions and shell's power. Each map will also be very well-suited to tank battles with covers and sniper positions to both sides to offer a fair-play match
